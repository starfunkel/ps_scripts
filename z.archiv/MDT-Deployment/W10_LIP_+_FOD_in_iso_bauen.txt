Language packs for Windows 10 install:

1. Mount LanguagePack.iso (SW_DVD9_NTRL_Win_10_1809_32_64_ARM64_MultiLang_LangPackAll_LIP_X21-91305.ISO)

2.mount wim Image:

dism.exe /mount-image "/imagefile:D:\MDTBUILDLAB\Operating Systems\WXLTSC2019\sources\install.wim" /index:1 /MountDir:D:\data\os_mount

3. Copy  cab from:

/x64/langpacks to D:\MDTBUILDLAB\Operating Systems\WXLTSC2019\Langpacks\ to 

D:\MDTBUILDLAB\Operating Systems\WXLTSC2019\Langpacks

create lang folder: e.g: \de-de

rename cab to lp.cab

4. check lang packs:

dism.exe /image:D:\mount "/distribution:D:\MDTBUILDLAB\Operating Systems\WXLTSC2019" /get-intl


5. recreate langINI:

dism.exe /image:d:\mount /gen-langINI "/distribution:D:\MDTBUILDLAB\Operating Systems\WXLTSC2019

6. unmount image:

dism.exe /unmount-image /mountdir:d:\mount /commit
